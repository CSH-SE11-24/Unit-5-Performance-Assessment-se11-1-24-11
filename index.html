The Loneliness epidemic

What is the Loneliness Epidemic

Loneliness is an emotional response. It's the difference between the social relationships people want and the social relationships people have. People are feeling more disconnected from others than ever before because of the lack of social connection.It's not a new problem though social connection has steadly decreasing but since the Social Movements in the 1960s & 1970s, it accelerated because local communities were losing their sense of importance.

Health Risks

According to The National Libaray of Medicine "Strong social relationships can increase the likelihood of survival by as much as 50% relative to individuals whose relationships are weaker. An increase in mortality has been correlated with both loneliness and social isolation" Loneliness can have health risks as well like shorter life span on average,higher blood pressure and higher risk of dementia statistically
